8 autumn foods suitable for the diet

When it comes to losing weight, diet plays a key role. Many people resort to extremes in their diet when trying to lose weight, but this is not necessary and even harmful. Instead, in the fall, you can make a bet on seasonal foods that are not only abundant, but also useful and can help you lose weight.

1. Apples.

Apples do not occupy the first place in the list for nothing. This is probably one of the fruits that can find the widest use in our kitchen. Apples are rich in many useful nutrients they are rich in nutrients and fiber, and they can be used to prepare a variety of dishes - from juice to main courses and desserts. These fruits are the best addition to the diet. Apples are low in calories and help control blood sugar levels. In addition, the low sodium content of apples helps prevent latency liquids.

2. Pumpkin.

Pumpkin is not only healthy, but also one of the best weight loss products that nutritionists recommend. It is low in calories and is an extremely good source of fiber, vitamins and minerals. In addition, pumpkin provides the body with one of the most important antioxidants - beta-carotene. With pumpkin, you can do almost anything, it is great for both soups and desserts.

3. Pumpkin seeds.

Do not throw away pumpkin seeds. Instead, dry them and bake them. These small seeds contain fiber, iron, copper, magnesium, phosphorus, protein, potassium, zinc, selenium. They contain monounsaturated fatty acids that help speed up your metabolism and burn fat.

4. Garnet.

Because of its small size, pomegranate has an extremely high fiber content, which, as you know, is very important for the weight loss process. Fiber for a long time retains the feeling of satiety and thus thus, it reduces cravings for harmful foods. Pomegranate is a great option for a snack.

5. Pears.

Pears are another autumn fruit that experts recommend for weight loss, because they are rich in fiber and help regulate the activity of the digestive system. A pear contains only 100 calories, which means that this makes it an ideal addition to a variety of dishes.

6. Cauliflower.

It is one of the best sources of antioxidants and is rich in vitamin C. in addition, half a Cup of cauliflower contains two grams of protein and two grams of fiber, which are important for effective weight loss. Rather than make mashed potatoes, make mashed cauliflower as a side dish to your favorite dishes.

7. Parsnips.

Very often, when dieting, people experience a feeling of hunger and insufficient satiety. Half a bowl of cooked parsnips contains more than three grams of fiber, which will help feel full. Parsnips have a high starch content, so experts believe that their consumption can replace that of bread or rice.

8. String beans.

This legume plant is a very good source of protein. Eating green beans will make you feel full for more for a long time, and you will not resort to your favorite harmful products. Beans are a great addition to many of the dishes you cook, and you can use them to diversify your diet.
