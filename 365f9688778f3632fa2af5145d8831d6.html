Useful properties of celery juice

Celery is a plant that belongs to the parsley and carrot family. Celery is the subject of research by scientists because of the presence of phenols and antioxidants in it. Celery juice contains fiber, potassium, vitamins A, C, and K, folic acid, and dozens of other antioxidants. In addition, this juice has much fewer calories than other juices.

Celery juice is suitable for relieving constipation, improving blood supply and detoxifying the body. Some useful properties of juice are listed below celery's.

Relieves constipation and helps with stomach problems.

Because of the abundance of fiber in celery, it can help deal with a number of stomach problems. Fiber is essential for good digestion, so taking celery juice can protect against constipation and irritable bowel syndrome intestine's.

Prevents cardiovascular problems.

Studies have found a strong Association between oxidative stress and hypertension. Eliminating free radicals that cause oxidative stress can help lower blood pressure. Celery juice contains flavonoids such as apigenin, which are potential antioxidants. In addition, celery leaf and seed extract can improve heart function by lowering blood cholesterol levels.

Anti-inflammatory properties.

Inflammation can occur for various reasons, such as physical stress, an allergic reaction or improper nutrition. In most cases, inflammation occurs due to a weak immune system. In other words, it occurs when the body cannot cope with stimuli from the external environment. Celery juice is recommended for people suffering from inflammatory diseases such as osteoarthritis, bronchitis, rheumatoid arthritis, asthma, gout, and others.

Reduces the risk of cancer.

As mentioned earlier, celery is a plant high in antioxidants, apigenin and luteolin, which are thought to have potentially anti-cancer properties. These antioxidants fight free radicals they also stimulate the process of autophagy, in which the body eliminates dysfunctional cells and thus reduces the risk of cancer.

Improving sleep.

A recent study shows that a plant-based diet improves sleep quality. Vegetables with a high content of polyphenols, such as celery, can modulate circadian rhythms and sleep patterns. Adding celery to Breakfast will not only improve it, but also give a good start to the day and stimulate brain activity. The antioxidants and minerals contained in celery protect brain cells from chemical stress.

It acts as a diuretic the tool and supports the health of the kidneys.

Celery juice contains two extremely important minerals-potassium and sodium. They regulate the amount of fluids in our body. Celery juice acts as a diuretic, thereby improving kidney health and reducing the risk of kidney infections.

Before before using celery juice, it is better to consult a specialist.
